Glass

Quality double glazing (if needed, combined with low-E surfaces) ensures that less heat is transferred trough the glass, therefore minimising the energy lost by air-condition.

Profile

A thermally and sound broken profile is almost equally important as glass, minimising the hear transfer from outside to inside.
