FIX

Fixed Windows open an effective and inexpensive way of allowing day light entering the house. They are best in areas where ventilation is not a big concern and natural light or a beautiful view needs to be maximized.

Casement

Our outward opening window are designed to have excellent performance and functionality during different season through the year: in high humidity, in dry conditions, while it is cold or hot. Gaskets and insulated glazing units used in the design ensure air tightness and energy efficacy.

T&T

Inward opening, especially Tilt and Turn windows, are the most modern and popular windows around the world. They are high secure, easy to clean, and available a lot of variations and designs.

Entrance

The entrance door is the central creative element in the front area of your home, acting as elegant visual connection between the exterior and the interior. Doors by German Windows are manufactured using top-quality panels or security glass only. Thanks to modern manufacturing technology a stylish front door does not have to be unaffordable anymore.

Lift & Slide

Q-Window’s lift & slide-doors allow you to create the generous interior design you need. The sliding-elements run quietly and easily, another special feature of this door. The particularly low threshold is ideal for barrier-free living. High-quality fittings guarantee secure functionality with high ease-of-use. A special sealing-system achieves optimal sealing, even in driving rain. Perfect freedom and a direct connection to the garden or the terrace.

Sliding & Folding

In the folding-sliding systems the “folding mechanism” practically allows you to open the entire opening width. The sash elements can be folded differently depending on the design version. No fixed glazed elements, sides or jambs block the opening. GERMAN made hardware allows sash weight up to 80 kg.

Balcony Door

Doors by German Windows are manufactured by using slim but strong profiles. Optimised thermal insulation is ensured through multi-chamber profiles and an optional thermally separated threshold. The steel reinforcements ensure maximum stability and long service life.
