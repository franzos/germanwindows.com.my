Maximize your home's potential

Doors provide connection – and separation. They add to our wellbeing and have a considerable impact on the style of our home. At Q-Windows, we offer a wide range of doors. Take advantage of our thousands of possible combinations and create a door whose design, function and quality are a perfect match for you and your lifestyle.

What does your perfect door look like?

You want doors that are as individual as a tailor-made suit – doors that complement your style and the way you live. Get inspired! But we all know that looks aren’t everything – what’s on the inside also counts.

  • 44 decors and lacquerings from trendy white and varied wood reproductions to exciting plain colours
  • 3 surfaces from hard-wearing CPL and de- cor foils to elegant lacquer
  • 84 door models from simple to classic, from country house to Design styles
  • 6 door frame options from round to square or pro led; for rebated and ush-closing doors with a great emphasis on design
  • 30 kinds of glass from transparent glass and structured glass to individual sandblasted glazing
  • 2 door edge options low-maintenance round edge or puristic square edge ( ush-closing doors always have a square edge)
